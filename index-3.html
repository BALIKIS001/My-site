About Me

Analyst. Thinker.
Problem Solver.

Hi! I'm Balikis Yussuf, a data analyst with a background in — and a genuine obsession with — finding patterns in places others overlook. I graduated from Lagos State University (LASU), and I've been building my skills in data analysis ever since.

I work primarily with Microsoft Excel and SQL to extract, clean, and interpret data. Whether it's spotting trends, building dashboards, or writing a well-structured query — I find the process deeply satisfying.

I'm currently growing my portfolio through real-world projects, and I believe every dataset tells a story worth sharing.
